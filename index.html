Our Story

Miku Sports Charity, Non-Profit Organization

Miku Sports Charity is a non-profit organization dedicated to making sports accessible to everyone. Founded in 2024, we aim to remove barriers to sports participation for underserved communities.

Our Mission

At Miku Sports Charity our mission is to let the fun of sports be accessible to everyone. We are committed to:

  • Promoting Inclusivity
  • Recruiting Volunteers

Chengwei Yan is a co-founding partner of Miku Sports Charity. Chengwei is passionate about harnessing the power of sports to create positive change and inclusivity.
